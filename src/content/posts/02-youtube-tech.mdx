## Plan your video

- Topic selection: solve a small, practical problem.
- Script: outline intro, steps, and conclusion.

## Recording and Editing

- Use OBS for screen recording.
- Edit with DaVinci Resolve or similar.

## Publish

- Write a detailed blog post with timestamps and code snippets.
- Embed the video in the blog post and add a short transcript.
